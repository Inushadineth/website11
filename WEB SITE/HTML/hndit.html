    Higher National Diploma in Information Technology (HNDIT)

   The Higher National Diploma in Information Technology (HND-IT) programme at the SriLanka Institute of Advance Technical Education (SLIATE) was developed and commenced in the year 2000 with the objective of producing the middle level IT professionals required for the new millennium.

Entry Qualifications

  1. School leavers with G.C.E (A/L) qualification in Mathematics, Bio Science or Commerce (Preferable) and Art stream.
  2. Minimum Simple Pass for English and Mathematics in G.C.E.(O/L).
